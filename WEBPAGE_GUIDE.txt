🎯 QUICK ACTIONS YOU CAN DO NOW:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ACTION 1: REGISTER NEW ACCOUNT
──────────────────────────────
1. Click "Register" button (top right)
2. You'll see a blue registration form
3. Fill in:
   ✏️  Username: (choose any unique name)
   ✏️  Email: your@email.com
   ✏️  Password: (min 8 characters)
   ✏️  Confirm Password: (same as password)
4. Click "Register" button
5. ✅ Success! You'll be redirected to login

ACTION 2: LOGIN TO YOUR ACCOUNT
───────────────────────────────
1. Click "Login" button (top right)
2. You'll see a login form
3. Enter:
   ✏️  Username: (your username)
   ✏️  Password: (your password)
4. Click "Sign In" button
5. ✅ Logged in! You're now in the dashboard

ACTION 3: VIEW ALL BLOGS
────────────────────────
1. Click "View Blogs" in navigation
2. You'll see list of all published blogs
3. Click on any blog to read full content
4. ✅ Blog reading experience!

ACTION 4: CREATE NEW BLOG POST
──────────────────────────────
1. Login first (if not logged in)
2. Click "Create Blog" in navigation
3. Fill in blog form:
   ✏️  Title: Your blog title
   ✏️  Content: Your blog content
   ✏️  Category: Select category
   ✏️  Tags: Add tags (optional)
4. Click "Publish" button
5. ✅ Blog published!
